Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

AURALiS ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

AURALiS is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary for the app to function:

  • Audio Files: Audio files you import are processed locally on your device for BPM detection and analysis. We do not upload your audio files to any server.
  • SoundCloud Integration: If you choose to connect your SoundCloud account, we store an authentication token locally on your device to access your SoundCloud data. We do not store your SoundCloud password.
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Analyze audio tracks for BPM, beat detection, and waveform generation
  • Access your SoundCloud library when you've authorized the connection
  • Remember your preferences and settings

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal information or audio files.

Third-Party Services

When you connect to SoundCloud, you are subject to SoundCloud's Privacy Policy. Please review their policy at soundcloud.com/pages/privacy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data stored locally on your device. Authentication tokens are stored securely using iOS Keychain.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your SoundCloud account at any time
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • Access all your data stored within the app

Children's Privacy

AURALiS is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at codingsharks@proton.me.
